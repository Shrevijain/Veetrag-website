Our Company

Veetrag Strufab, has been in the business of steel processing and fabrication since 2019 and has experienced supervisory and management personnel with the expertise to maintain our excellent reputation for quality workmanship and meeting schedules. We are constantly striving to improve our services and products to better serve our customers. Our customer base is broad in all types of industrial requirements. Technonogical steel fabrication is being furnished to Process Plants, Power plants, Sugar Plants, chemical plants and other industrial applications. Veetrag Strufab, is a creative structural steel and equipment fabrication company. We are proud to serve clients throughout India. The company, is formed by a team of Technocrats, having a long technical and manufacturing experience for over 21 years, and being OEM/suppliers, our Quality is well acclaimed in the market which is evident from our rapidly increasing clientele.

Our Services

What we Offer

Structural Steel Fabrication

Steel Fabrication (Light, Medium & Heavy) & Machining, Forgings, and Castings with World class suppliers within India. This starts from raw material procurement to despatch & installation at site execution by customer.

Machining & Stress Relieving

We have full service tie up with various workshop to carry-out machining, stress relieving activity. We offers Testing of Steel, Welding via non destructive & destructive.

Handrail & Grating Fabrication

We do manufacturing and supply of Grating, Chequered Plate, Handrails & Safety Guards.

Steel Fabrication

We offers steel fabrication drafting and designing for Industrial project. It includes Marking, identification, Steel Crating and Packing of finished goods.

Hot Dip Galvanising

Veetrag Strufab, is a custom carbon steel fabrication company and offers galvanising as out sourced and having tie up with various shops

Blast Cleaning & painting

We offers blasting as per sweedish standard and Air Pressured painting of various types. This also include Surface Preparation-Hand tool Cleaning.

Acoustics System & Its Components

We do manufacturing, Supply and Installation of Acoustics System & Its Components.

Supply of Fabricated items

Supply of Boughtouts Components like Hardwares, Flanges, Gear Boxes, Motors, Belts, Rubber sheets and Fabricated items- Conveyor Frames, Chutes, Wear plates (Fabricated, Casting), etc. based on Customers Drawing or requirement.

Conveyor Bucket Elevators, Dampers, Expansion Joints

We do Supply of Conveyor (Chain, Screw, Belt type) Bucket Elevators, Dampers, Expansion Joints as per customer requirement.
